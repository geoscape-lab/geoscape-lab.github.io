About GeoScape Lab

GeoScape Lab is an independent research collective, a meeting point for scholars and professionals who think across disciplines, institutions, and borders.

We study the environment as a living system, shaped as much by rivers and rainfall as by roads, cities, and the choices people make. Our curiosity moves freely: across water and land, transport and air, resources and energy, climate and community. Wherever the natural world meets the human one, we find questions worth asking.

Our work travels from South Asia to regions across the Global South and beyond, because the pressures we study know no borders.

We work with GIS, remote sensing, Google Earth Engine, machine learning, GeoAI, geostatistics, modelling, and fieldwork. A broad toolkit for a broad world.
