Join GeoScape Lab

We welcome motivated researchers who share our passion for geospatial science and environmental problem-solving.

Current Vacancies

There are no vacancies at this time. Please check back later or reach out to express your interest for future opportunities.

Who We Are Looking For

GeoScape Lab is an open, collaborative research community. We welcome applications from students and researchers at any career stage, whether undergraduate, postgraduate, or early-career professional, who are excited about using geospatial tools to understand environmental change.

We particularly encourage applications from researchers with interests in:

  • Disaster risk assessment and flood hazard analysis
  • Land use / land cover change and remote sensing
  • Water resource dynamics and hydrological modelling
  • Environmental economics and ecosystem valuation
  • Climate change vulnerability and adaptation planning
  • Machine learning and GeoAI for spatial applications

What We Offer

As part of GeoScape Lab, you will:

  • Work alongside a diverse group of researchers from multiple institutions
  • Gain hands-on experience with GIS, remote sensing platforms (Google Earth Engine, QGIS, ArcGIS), and ML tools
  • Contribute to real, ongoing research projects with publication potential
  • Develop academic writing and scientific communication skills
  • Build a professional network across disciplines and institutions

Positions at GeoScape Lab are collaborative and research-focused. As an independent lab, we do not currently offer stipends or formal fellowships, but we provide a rigorous, mentored research environment where your work will be valued and recognised.

What We Expect

We expect prospective members to be self-motivated, intellectually curious, and committed to producing rigorous, honest science. You should be comfortable working independently while also being a collaborative team member. Some familiarity with GIS software, Python, or R is an advantage, though it is not a strict requirement; we value a strong research mindset above technical credentials.

How to Apply

To express your interest, please send an email to the lab at geoscape.lab@gmail.com (also listed on the Contact page) with the following:

  • A brief statement of interest (no more than one page) describing your background and why you want to join GeoScape Lab
  • Your CV or résumé
  • A sample of your previous work (thesis chapter, report, map, or code, anything that shows your capabilities)
  • Contact details for one academic or professional reference

We review applications on a rolling basis and aim to respond within two to three weeks.

Ready to Apply?

Send your application materials to the lab email. We look forward to hearing from you and will do our best to respond promptly. We accept applications year-round, with no fixed deadline.
